Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Events.com?

    Events.com is an easy-to-use platform where you can discover, register for, and manage your participation in events from workshops and webinars to large conferences and community gatherings.

  • How much does it cost to use Events.com?

    Creating an account is free! Some events are free to attend, while others may have a registration fee set by the organizers. You’ll always see the price upfront before registering.

  • Where can I register and join events?

    You can register from anywhere using your phone, tablet, or computer. Once registered, you’ll receive all event details, including online access links or venue directions.

  • How do I cancel or change my registration?

    You can manage your registrations through your account dashboard. Just click “Cancel” or “Edit” on the event page. Note: Cancellation policies may vary depending on the event organizer.

  • What types of events can I find on Events.com?

    We feature a wide range of events: tech talks, creative workshops, professional conferences, fitness classes, cultural experiences, and more—both online and in-person.
